The best way to turn paper forms into digital data.

Captricity is an award-winning Berkeley, Calif.-based technology startup that offers a completely self-serve data entry service for digitizing paper forms. Captricity can get data off of paper and into a digital spreadsheet quickly and cheaply. It provides human-quality data entry that even works on hand-writing -- but at a fraction of what manual data entry costs.

Manage and Measure Your Social Media Outreach.

Measured Voice is a web-based social media management system (SMMS) designed to help government organizations create, publish, and measure social media content. Identify and build a community of active contributors.

MindMixer is an engagement tool that activates community contributors. Ideas, voices, and perspectives are shared by the community and help to facilitate deeper conversations that yield actionable insights. The tool has helped create a network of contributors in more than 250 organizations around the country.

Help towns organize disaster recovery with mobile and web-based technology.

Recovers is building a toolkit for community powered disaster recovery. Recovers disaster response platform incorporates searchable databases, social media aggregation, and mobile tools, allowing grassroots organizers to efficiently marshal resources in their area. Recovers provides vital infrastructure to a devastated area, even if internet is knocked out for a week.

recovers.org

Revelstone is a performance metrics and social networking company for the public sector.
